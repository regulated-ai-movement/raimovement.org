# Artificial Intelligence Restrictions as Licenses (AIR)

The Artificial Intelligence Restrictions (AIR) are a set of licenses designed to protecting various type of content, and controlling how it can be used in conjunction with Artificial Intelligence.

## License Types

At the moment we defined 2 types of licenses:
- **AIR for Software (AIR-S)**: This license is designed to protect and control human produced software from AI Entities. It's inspired by the [MIT License](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT) and the [Apache License](https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0).
- **AIR for Creative Commons (AIR-CC)**: As the name suggests, this license is designed to protect and control human produced content from AI Entities. It's inspired by the [Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-NoDerivatives 4.0 International Public License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-nd/4.0/legalcode).
