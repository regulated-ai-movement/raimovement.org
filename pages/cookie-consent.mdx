# Cookie Consent Notice

Our website, raimovement.org, respects your privacy.

While we do not actively use cookies ourselves to track your visit to our website, our site does make use of Google Analytics, a service which transmits website traffic data to Google servers.

## What is a cookie?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your machine to help the site provide a better user experience. In general, cookies are used to retain user preferences, store information for things like shopping carts, and provide anonymized tracking data to third party applications like Google Analytics.

## What does Google Analytics do?

Google Analytics is a web analysis service provided by Google Inc. (“Google”). Google utilizes the Data collected to track and examine the use of this website, to prepare reports on its activities and share them with other Google services. Google may use the Data collected to contextualize and personalize the ads of its own advertising network.

This data is not tied to personally identifiable information. We do not use this data to identify users personally. Our purpose in using analytics is to understand how well our site is working, how people are finding it, and what content is being viewed. This allows us to improve the service we provide to you.
